FAQ

Questions, answered.

How far in advance should I book? +

For weddings, we recommend booking 6–9 months in advance, especially for peak season (April–October). Corporate events can often be accommodated on shorter notice — call us at 469-478-7110 to check availability.

What areas do you service? +

We're based in Dallas and serve the entire DFW metroplex with no travel fees. We also travel statewide and nationally for corporate activations and destination weddings — additional travel rates apply outside DFW.

Do guests get unlimited prints? +

Yes. Every package includes unlimited prints throughout your event window. We recommend a 2x6 strip for weddings (everyone gets one) and 4x6 single prints for corporate events.

Can you brand the booth for our company? +

Absolutely. Our corporate package includes full brand customization — logos on prints, custom backdrops, branded overlays, and even printed step-and-repeats. T-Mobile, for example, gets a fully branded experience at every activation.

How much space do you need? +

A 10ft x 10ft area is ideal for our open-air setup with backdrop. We can work in slightly smaller spaces — let us know your venue and we'll confirm ahead of time.

When do we get the digital photos? +

Within 24 hours of your event. We deliver a private online gallery you can share with guests, download in full resolution, or post to social media.
